Understanding Stress and Strain in Mechanical Engineering

In mechanical engineering, the concepts of stress and strain are fundamental to analyzing and designing structures and materials. Understanding how materials behave under various loads is crucial for ensuring safety and performance.

What is Stress?

Stress is defined as the internal force experienced by a material per unit area due to externally applied forces. It quantifies how much force a material can withstand before deforming or failing.
